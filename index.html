Climate Team-1

Climate Change is a growing problem in the world. To meet the SDG goal on climate action, it is necessary to promote renewable sources of energy, and while at it, ensure that they are efficiently installed to minimize their contribution towards climate chnage. In this project, we have created a visualization site based on data to showcase the emissions by the respective African countries and energy sources.

To accomplish this, we utilized ** the OpenWeatherMap API, Pandas, and Matplotlib to create and visualize a representative dataset of CO2 emissions acrross Africa.

The objective was to build a series of chats to showcase the following:

  1. Emissions from Hydropower Generation
  2. Emissions from Thermal(Gas&Diesel) Power Generation
  3. Emissions from Nuclear Power Generation
  4. Emissions from Coal in Power Generation
